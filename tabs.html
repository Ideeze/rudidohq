Who we serve

Entrepreneurship enabling organizations

Rudido provides the support to innovators, creatives, content creators, & entrepreneurs. Basically anyone with an idea for a startup.

Rudido offers entrepreneurship-enabling leaders and organizations access to entrepreneurial research data, and the opportunity to conveniently, source, onboard, educate, and invest in credible innovators remotely.

Features

  • Content-Portfolio Books
  • Virtual Workspaces
  • Innovation Marketplace
  • Budgeting & Investments

Create portfolio books to showcase your different content, skills, & learning materials.

Save all your research notes, audio & video files, images, links and messages directly into your content portfolio book. Organize and manage all your idea’s content in one place.

Acess a virtual workspace to ideate and work remotely with your team & partners.

Access research coaching tips and share progress reports with friends, collegues & partners. Upload your research notes, ideas, skills & learning materials to your content portfolio-books to share with your audience.

Find the right people to partner with or hire for your ideas.

Source for content, learning materials & people seamlessly. Promote your content, learning materials, skills, ideas, businesses and tools to the right audience.

Access the past & present financial history & milestones of innovators.

Access funds from people & businesses that care about your ideas. Access fundraising coaching tips and share milestones reports with donors.
